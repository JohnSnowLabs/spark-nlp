## Description

This is a pretrained pipeline to extract Companies (ORG), People (PERSON), Job titles (ROLE) and Dates combining different pretrained NER models to improve coverage.

## Predicted Entities

`ORG`, `PERSON`, `ROLE`, `DATE`
