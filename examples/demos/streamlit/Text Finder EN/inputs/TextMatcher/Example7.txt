Dow rallies more than 200 points after biggest selloff since March. Stocks were hammered on Thursday as fears about an increase in coronavirus cases and a somber economic outlook from the US Federal Reserve forced investors to confront risks that had been pushed aside during the huge market rally that has taken place since March. But fear of missing out is a powerful motivator to buy stocks, and the US market was set for a rebound.
